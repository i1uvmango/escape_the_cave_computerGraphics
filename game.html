ESCAPE THE CAVE

You're trapped in a collapsed cave. Find the 3 key fragments, then reach the exit. Goblins lurk in the dark and are drawn to your flashlight — light the way, but don't get cornered.
TIP — Your flashlight has only 3 minutes of battery (it drains only while ON, left-click to toggle). Use it sparingly and light your path with glowstones instead.
